# Thesis Breaker Japan — Submission

**Tagline:** Know when the evidence has broken your thesis.

**Category:** Apps for Your Life  
**Codex Session ID:** `019f7306-7262-7371-a03f-6b99df7129bf`

## What it does

The product turns a Japanese equity thesis into measurable claims with explicit
break conditions, checks new evidence, cites the relevant document page, and
flags the thesis that must be reviewed.

## How it was built

I used Codex with GPT-5.6 to implement thesis structures, two consecutive
quarter-to-quarter transition checks over three quarterly observations, page
citations, tests, and the browser interface. A future live path can use GPT-5.6
to propose structured claims from filings; the public demo uses synthetic
tested filings, and investors verify all source documents.

## Proof

- three theses evaluated
- one thesis broken after two consecutive declines
- every result carries a document-page reference
