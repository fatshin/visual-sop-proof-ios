Thesis Breaker Japan looks for evidence that disproves an investment thesis before evidence that supports it. The fixture contains three explicit theses and three adjacent normalized quarters with page-level source anchors whose metric values are verified. Running the evaluator breaks the overseas-revenue thesis after two consecutive declines, moves that broken thesis to the top, and leaves the margin and free-cash-flow theses intact. This rule is stateless and evaluates only the latest three-quarter window, so one flat or rising latest comparison makes that window intact. Invalid quarter labels, gaps, duplicates, non-finite metrics, unknown rules, and source-value mismatches fail closed. I used Codex with GPT-5.6 to define falsification rules, implement the deterministic evaluator, generate adversarial tests, and review the financial disclaimer. This product does not provide financial advice or place trades.
