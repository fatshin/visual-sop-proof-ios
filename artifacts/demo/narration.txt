Body Recomposition Evidence Engine separates useful weight loss from strength loss. This privacy-safe fixture combines thirty days of synthetic HealthKit-style weight and nutrition data, Strong workout records for three lifts, and two body scans. The local analysis shows weight and body fat trending down while lean mass and all three estimated one-repetition maximums improve. Raw health observations are never sent to OpenAI. I used Codex with GPT-5.6 to implement EWMA and estimated one-repetition maximum calculations, write privacy tests, and review the medical disclaimer. A live GPT-5.6 extension is limited to summaries over derived aggregates.
