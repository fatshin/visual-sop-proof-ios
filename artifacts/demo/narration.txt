Body Recomposition Evidence Engine separates useful fat loss from strength loss, even when the scale looks discouraging. This privacy-safe fixture combines thirty distinct consecutive days of synthetic HealthKit-style weight and nutrition data, two distinct workout records for each required lift, and two distinct body scans. Daily weight rises, falls, and plateaus, while the exponentially weighted trend and body-fat percentage move down. Lean mass and all three estimated one-repetition maximums improve, so the result can say that strength was preserved. A date gap, duplicate observation, empty scan set, missing lift, or declining lift returns review needed before any reassuring classification. Raw health observations stay local and are never sent to OpenAI. I used Codex with GPT-5.6 to implement the calculations, write fail-closed and privacy tests, and review the medical disclaimer.
