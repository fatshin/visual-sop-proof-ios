Thesis Breaker Japan looks for the evidence that disproves an investment thesis before the evidence that supports it. The fixture contains three explicit theses and three normalized quarters with page-level source labels. Running the evaluator breaks the overseas-revenue thesis after two consecutive declines, while the margin and free-cash-flow theses remain intact. I used Codex with GPT-5.6 to define falsification rules, implement the two-period trigger, generate tests, and review the financial disclaimer. A live GPT-5.6 extension can extract candidate claims from normalized filings, but deterministic rules decide status. This product does not provide financial advice or place trades.
